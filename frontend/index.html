How it works

01

Browser fetch

Playwright opens a real Chromium browser and loads your URL, including all JavaScript. This is why it works on modern sites.

02

Content extraction

The page HTML is cleaned and stripped of navigation, scripts, and boilerplate. Product cards and tables are extracted with a structured parser.

03

LLM reasoning

The cleaned text and all page links are sent to Groq (llama-3.3-70b). The model decides: extract the data, navigate to another page, or report not found.

04

Navigate and repeat

If the model says navigate, the agent follows that link and repeats from step one. It continues until it finds the data or exhausts useful paths.

05

Structured output

All extracted items are returned as a table. Every row includes the source URL. Download as CSV, Excel, or JSON.
